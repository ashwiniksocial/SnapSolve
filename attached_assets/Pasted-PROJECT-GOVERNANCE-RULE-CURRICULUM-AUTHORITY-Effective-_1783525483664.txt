PROJECT GOVERNANCE RULE — CURRICULUM AUTHORITY

Effective immediately, all curriculum decisions, syllabus verification, chapter audits, chapter creation, curriculum compliance checks, content validation, curriculum migrations, rationalization reviews, and academic-gap analyses must use ONLY official Government of India educational sources.

AUTHORITATIVE SOURCES

Primary:

- NCERT (https://ncert.nic.in)
- CBSE Academic (https://cbseacademic.nic.in)

Secondary (allowed only when directly quoting official government publications):

- Official NCERT textbook PDFs
- Official CBSE curriculum PDFs
- Official CBSE circulars
- Official NCERT notifications
- Ministry of Education publications
- Government of India education portals

NON-AUTHORITATIVE SOURCES

The following must NEVER be used as evidence for curriculum decisions:

- Educart
- Oswaal
- Competishun
- myCBSEguide
- coaching institutes
- blogs
- YouTube channels
- media summaries
- third-party syllabus websites
- exam-preparation portals
- AI-generated summaries

These sources may be used only to discover a possible change, but never as evidence.

MANDATORY VERIFICATION RULE

Any claim that:

- a chapter was added
- a chapter was removed
- a chapter was renamed
- a subject was restructured
- a textbook changed
- a curriculum was rationalized

must be supported by:

1. Official source URL
2. Publication date
3. Exact document title
4. Direct chapter evidence

Otherwise the claim must be classified:

UNVERIFIED

and no implementation decision may be taken from it.

IMPLEMENTATION GATE

No curriculum authoring, deletion, migration, remapping, or restructuring may proceed until official-source verification is complete.

When evidence is insufficient, default action is:

FREEZE CHANGES AND REQUEST OFFICIAL VERIFICATION.

This rule applies to:
- curriculum audits
- chapter authoring
- syllabus compliance checks
- gateway validations
- content reviews
- future subject expansion

Priority: Highest
Override: None
