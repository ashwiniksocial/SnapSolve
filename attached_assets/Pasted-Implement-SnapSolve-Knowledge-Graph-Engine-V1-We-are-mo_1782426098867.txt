Implement **SnapSolve Knowledge Graph Engine V1**.

We are moving from topic-based learning to concept-based learning.

Every chapter, topic and question in SnapSolve must become part of one connected knowledge graph.

## Concept Graph

Represent every concept as a node.

Each node contains

id

subject

board

class

chapter

topic

concept

difficulty

importance

estimated learning time

common misconceptions

prerequisites

successors

related concepts

exam frequency

NCERT references

question ids

revision priority

---

## Dependency Engine

Every concept should know

what it depends on

and

what depends on it.

Examples

Fractions

↓

Integers

↓

Algebra

↓

Linear Equations

↓

Polynomials

↓

Quadratic Equations

---

## Concept Mastery

Track mastery per concept instead of only per chapter.

Mastery should automatically propagate.

If Algebra mastery drops,

Linear Equations and Polynomials should also be affected.

---

## Prerequisite Inference

When a student repeatedly fails,

walk backwards through the graph.

Identify the first missing prerequisite.

Recommend repairing only that concept.

---

## Learning Path

Instead of recommending topics,

recommend concepts.

Generate the shortest learning path from

Current Mastery

to

Target Exam Score.

---

## Remediation

Automatically build a personalised recovery plan.

Teach prerequisite

↓

Practice prerequisite

↓

Verify prerequisite

↓

Return to original topic

---

## Concept Search

Allow AI to search by concept rather than chapter.

Example

Student asks

"Why do we divide here?"

Search

Ratio

Fraction

Probability

instead of chapter names.

---

## Teacher Goal

The tutor should understand

HOW all concepts connect.

Not simply which chapter they belong to.

This engine becomes the foundation for every recommendation, explanation and revision plan.
