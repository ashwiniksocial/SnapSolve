==================================================
QUESTION BANK POLICY
==================================================

Legacy question banks are NOT deleted.

They become candidate repositories only.

A legacy question may be reused ONLY IF:

1. it is individually verified against the uploaded official NCERT/CBSE PDFs;

2. it belongs to the exact official subject;

3. it belongs to the exact official chapter;

4. it belongs to the correct topic within that chapter;

5. it does not introduce concepts outside the official scope.

If any doubt exists,

DO NOT REUSE.

Create new content instead.
