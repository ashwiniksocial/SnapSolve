====================================================

The reviewer must evaluate

1.

CURRICULUM

Verify

- Latest official NCERT/CBSE curriculum
- Correct chapter
- Correct topic
- Correct terminology
- Correct learning outcome
- No deleted syllabus
- No out-of-syllabus content

2.

QUESTION QUALITY

Verify

- Correct concept
- Appropriate difficulty
- Clear
- Unambiguous
- Tests intended learning objective
- Matches current CBSE style

3.

ANSWER CORRECTNESS

Verify

- Final answer
- Formulae
- Calculations
- Units
- Scientific facts
- Definitions
- Mathematical logic
- Grammar
- Internal consistency
- No contradictions
- No hallucinations

4.

TEACHING QUALITY

Determine whether an average or weak student will genuinely understand the topic.

Verify

- WHY before HOW
- Every reasoning step explained
- No skipped logic
- Simple English
- Difficult words explained
- Important concepts highlighted
- Common mistakes addressed
- Key takeaway present
- Student can solve a similar question afterwards

5.

EXAM READINESS

Determine whether the Standard Answer would receive FULL MARKS in the current CBSE Board Examination.

Verify

- Correct terminology
- Covers all marking points
- Appropriate structure
- Appropriate detail
- No unnecessary information

6.

COMPACT ANSWER

Verify

- Suitable for revision
- Complete
- Concise
- Easy to revise quickly

7.

STANDARD ANSWER

Verify

- Ideal Board Examination answer
- Balanced explanation
- Examiner-friendly
- Full-mark answer

8.

DETAILED ANSWER

Verify

- Complete teaching lesson
- Builds intuition
- Explains reasoning
- Removes misconceptions
- Deep conceptual understanding

ONE

If they still fail,

mark the Question as

Needs Review
